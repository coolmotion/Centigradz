Our Story

CENTIGRADZ, a revered presence in Sri Lanka's music scene, has earned global acclaim, inspiring budding musicians. Originating as four college students with a shared dream, they explored diverse genres like Pop, RnB, and Alternative. In 2002, their early singles, "Tradition" and "Dark Angel," skyrocketed to success. "Tradition" claimed the #2 spot on Sri Lanka's hot 40 chart, while "Dark Angel" reached #1 within just 24 hours. Their debut album, "Heritage" (2005), set records as Sri Lanka's fastest-selling debut, with iconic music videos like "Dark Angel" and "Natamu," directed by Pravin Jayarathne, dominating charts for 15 weeks in 2008.

CENTIGRADZ expanded into movie soundtracks and appeared in films like "Heart Fm." Their accolades extended to representing Sri Lanka at the prestigious "Asia Pacific World Star Quest," where they won the coveted "Excellent New Singer Award." Despite challenges, CENTIGRADZ's unwavering passion for music led to over 500 sold-out shows, forging deep bonds with their dedicated fan base. Their commitment to charitable causes and their unique ability to uplift spirits through their enchanting music have left an indelible mark on their enduring musical legacy.

Our Story

CENTIGRADZ excels in Sri Lanka's music industry, inspiring young talents. Four college students with a dream began their journey by covering popular bands. In 2002, they recorded "Tradition" and "Dark Angel," becoming instant hits in 2003. "Tradition" hit #2 on Sri Lanka's chart, and "Dark Angel" climbed to #1 on ABC Network's Sun Fm Hot 40 Countdown within 24 hours, holding the top spot for four weeks. Their debut album "Heritage" in 2005 soared in the charts. Their success continued in music videos, starting with "Sulange Se" in 2006 and more hits in 2006/2007 and 2008.

Their triumphs led them to create theme songs for movies and even act. Representing Sri Lanka in the "Asia Pacific World Star Quest," they earned accolades. With 500 sold-out shows, they dominate ticket sales. Their performances, cherished by loyal fans, support charitable causes. CENTIGRADZ's journey is an inspiration to the music community. They began humbly, covering bands and gaining appreciation. The success of "Tradition" and "Dark Angel" marked their rise, and their presence in music videos solidified it. Their venture into movies showcased their versatility. Representing Sri Lanka internationally, they gained recognition. Their performances resonate with fans, leading to sold-out shows. Beyond music, their commitment to charity highlights their desire to spread joy through their magical sound.
